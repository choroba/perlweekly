PerlModules.net now sends emails!

by Alexander Karelas (KARJALA)

PerlModules.net is a site that notifies you whenever your favorite Perl modules get updated. Up till now it only provided RSS feeds, but from now on you can also get E-mail notifications. I wonder if that is going to be it, or will there be other forms of notifications. E.g. on IRC. Twitter, Slack?
