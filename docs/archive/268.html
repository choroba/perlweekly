Hi there!

There are not a lot of perl-related articles this week, so let me use the opportunity to mention my new 'show' again. I would like to provide a platform for people to hear about interesting open source projects and for others to provide news about their projects. That's the CMOS show.

In its core it is a podcast, but most of the episodes also come with a video recording and to allow people who can't hear or cannot understand the accents spoken, there is also a transcript of each episode.

Let me invite you to subscribe to the podcast and if you have suggestions who to interview, let me know.

Enjoy your week!

Gabor Szabo
