Hi there,

Please accept my apology for sharing a crypto scam link in the 716th edition of the newsletter. Thanks to Gabor and Dave, it was removed straight away. I will be more cautious from now onwards, promise.

Recently, I stumbled upon a post titled "Top 11 Perl Services Companies in the World in 2025". Sadly, not a single one is based in my city London. There was a time, we had plenty! Still, it's not all bad. I was thrilled to see ActiveState on the list, the company behind ActiveState Perl.

In an earlier edition of the newsletter, I mentioned my wish to attend the Perl Toolchain Summit which is an invite-only annual event. I also shared my interest in contributing to the MetaCPAN project. To my absolute delight, that wish came true the very same day! I received an invitation from the organiser to join the Perl Toolchain Summit 2025, happening in Leipzig, Germany from May 1st to May 4th 2025. I didn't wait even a second to accept the invitation. It's truly an honour to be part of such a talented group.

In all the excitement, I've already booked my flight ticket to Berlin and from there I'll hop on a fast train to Leipzig. The last time, I visited Germany was back in 2018 when I attended the 20th German Perl Workshop in the city of Gummersbach. I even gave couple of talks and one of them being "Introduction to Map::Tube". After the event, I even shared my experience in this event report.

Enjoy rest of the newsletter.

Mohammad Sajid Anwar
